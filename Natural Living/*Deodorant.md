#### Ingredients:
- 1/4 cup (60 grams) baking soda (reduce to 2 tablespoons if you have sensitive skin)
- 1/4 cup (30 grams) arrowroot powder (or cornstarch if arrowroot is not available)
- 1/4 cup (60 grams) coconut oil (solid at room temperature)
- 10 drops of your preferred essential oil (e.g., tea tree, lavender, lemon, eucalyptus, peppermint) (optional, for fragrance and additional antibacterial properties)
